Career Paths

For many people entering leaving high school, college is the obvious option. For CS majors there are a few career paths available. These are Data Science, Cybersecurity, AI, UI/UX, Web Development, Software Development, Network Engineering, and more. See the CS Careers Page for a more in-depth overview of CS careers.

The trades are a massive field with a large variety of jobs across several industries. But some of the higher paying ones include Elevator Technician, Radiation Therapist, Dental Hygienist, Sonographer, Electrician, and more. See the Trades Careers Page for a more in-depth overview of Trade careers.
