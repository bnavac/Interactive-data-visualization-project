Employment Statistics

Un, Under, and Employment statistics from the department of labour. Unemployment is the portion of people who have a degree, training, or worked their last job in the field and do not have a job.

Underemployment is the portion of people who have a job but their job does not require a degree. High underemployment in a field means that people with a degree are unable to find jobs in their field and are forced into low skill work.

Employment is the portion of people who have a job that requires a bachelors degree or the highest form of training they have.
