Income

The biggest metric that people look for in a career is income. It is the metric that turns heads when they hear things like “6 figures”. However, not every career is made equal, if you are a college student you probably do not work or work infrequent or low-income jobs. Meanwhile, tradespeople will have a shorter education period before they start full-time work. We reflect this in the data by having a 5-year delay between when a trades-person would begin work and when a college student would graduate and begin work. This is an estimate based on past data and future projections to determine the income for each career.
