Career Paths

For many people entering leaving high school, college is the obvious option. To CS majors there are a few career paths available. These are data science, cybersecurity, AI, UI/UX, Web Development, Embedded Development, Network engineering, and more. See the CS careers page for a more in-depth overview of CS careers.
