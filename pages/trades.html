Trade School Careers

The kind and type of careers that stem from trade school can vary wildly, with some you can do with only a certificate or a few weeks of training and others requiring apprenticeships and union memberships. But here are a few.

Elevator installers and repairers, also called elevator constructors or elevator mechanics, assemble, install, maintain, and replace elevators, escalators, chairlifts, moving walkways, and similar equipment in buildings. Elevator installers and repairers usually specialize in installation, maintenance, or repair work.

A radiation therapist is part of the care team that treats patients with cancer and other diseases through the use of radiation treatments. They work with doctors who specialize in radiation therapy (radiation oncologists) and oncology nurses who specialize in caring for cancer patients. Radiation therapy is used to treat cancer by shrinking or removing cancer or tumors.

There are of course, many more, but this shows that the trades is not all about manual labour and spans many industries.
