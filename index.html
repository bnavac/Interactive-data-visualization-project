CS vs Trade School

It is common advice for people who cannot find jobs after graduating college or even for high school students who are unsure about what they want to do, to “do a trade.” Or get a certification, enter trade school, or complete vocational school and find a job in that field due to a lack of tradespeople. However, there is an equal number of people promoting STEM degrees, particularly CS and engineering due to the high pay and job growth.

So which do you pick, college, like hundreds of thousands of your peers, and try for the six-figure white collar job? Or do you go to trade or vocation school or get training or a certificate, and get a trades job?

We hope to provide some clarity on the answer, see the about page for our methodology and the careers path page for further insight into the two career paths.
